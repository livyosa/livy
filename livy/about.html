About Me

Front-End Developer & UX Designer based in Los Angeles

I’m Olivia Tang (but you can call me Liv). I’m passionate about crafting clean, functional, and emotionally resonant web experiences.

After transitioning from behavioral work into tech, I’ve combined empathy-driven design with front-end development to bring ideas to life. I love designing flows that feel intuitive and human.

Tools I use regularly: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Figma, and Photoshop. When I’m not coding, I’m probably lifting weights, sketching ideas, or watching animations frame by frame.
